# nessus-to-excel-nte
NtE is a 100% local Windows app that converts .nessus files into customizable Excel dashboards in just 2 clicks. Enjoy a modern drag-and-drop UI, complete data privacy (zero cloud), and dynamic reporting for Compliance &amp; vulnerability scans.


See for yourself, with screenshots included, how NtE transforms your .nessus files into usable Excel deliverables in just a few clicks.

Get started right away
Drag and drop your .nessus file onto the interface—NtE takes care of the rest. The streamlined interface gives you access to all features with no learning curve.

Two types of scans, one interface
Whether it's a CIS compliance audit or a vulnerability scan, NtE automatically detects the type of file being imported and adjusts its settings accordingly.

Your reports your rules
NtE extracts 100% of the data from the source file. Choose what appears in the report: hide IP addresses, add a column to provide context for certain results—the deliverable adapts to your specific needs.
<img width="7138" height="2884" alt="image(2)" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ee4de309-b28b-4944-9d9b-be82721ee63f" />

Your settings, once and for all
Language, theme, display mode, presets by scan type: set them up once, generate without having to revisit them. Your preferences are saved from one session to the next.

Professional deliverables
NtE generates an Excel file along with a dashboard that matches your configuration and dynamically adapts to any changes you make after generation.
Instantly view the breakdown of results by compliance level and severity. Key metrics (number of hosts scanned, total number of non-compliances, compliance rate) provide a concise overview of the audited security posture.

View the distribution of vulnerabilities by severity, key metrics for your environment, and a Top 10 list of the most common vulnerabilities to help you immediately prioritize your remediation efforts.
